City By Fate

Genre: City-Building Sim / Adult Narrative

Platform: Windows / Linuxt

Status: In Development

Overview

One of the cities of Eros has asked the God of Fate for help. Their citizens have grown restless and unhappy. But with his hands full quelling the lingering turmoil from the Last Country War, he sent you in his place.

As the appointed Overseer, you'll guide this struggling city toward prosperity - or ruin. Shape their infrastructure, enfore your will, and manage both desires and disobedience. Will they worship you as a savior? Or curse your name as their world collapses?

Features

  • NSFW Pixel art simulation with moral choices and branching scenarios.li>

  • Erotic encounters woven into the city's political and social balance.
